Bodhi Mind Meditations

Enhance your life with the Bodhi Mind app.



We’ll help you become the best possible version of you through the power of meditation and mindfulness.


Bodhi Mind comes with a free two week trial in which you can access more than 200 meditations led by Bodhipaksa, an acclaimed meditation teacher. These cover everything from a basic introduction to the practice of mindfulness, which will help you to have a healthier and happier life, through meditations on lovingkindness and compassion, to more advanced insight meditations.


After the trial you will still have access to 12 free meditations for the life of the app. To keep the rest unlocked, you'll want to choose one of our subscription packages.


Our library of meditations will continually grow. But right from the very start you’ll find meditations to help you deal with stress and anxiety, to help you sleep better, and to help you overcome self-criticism.


Meditation packages from our online courses will help you create healthier relationships with people in your life — and with yourself. There are even meditations that are only two or three minutes long that will help you fit mindfulness practice into the busiest of days.


Work your way through a structured program of meditations, browse categories such as “Stress” or “Self-Compassion” to pick the meditation that best fits your needs, or try out the latest offerings from the list of recently added meditations.


You can create a playlist of your favorite tracks, and see which meditations you’ve played most often.


Sit back, relax, and let Bodhi Mind guide you toward peace of mind. The next step of your journey of self-discovery is just a download away.
